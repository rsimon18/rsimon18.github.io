Who I Am

I am a forward thinking UX Designer who challenges convention and puts user needs above all else.

My specialty is in architecting and designing responsive, mobile first products. I also am experienced in designing mobile and tablet apps and interactive touch screen displays.

Through thorough research and an iterative process, I produce functional and enjoyable digital experiences for both clients and consumers. I collaborate with project managers, graphic designers, and developers to create unique experiences for Fortune 500 businesses.
